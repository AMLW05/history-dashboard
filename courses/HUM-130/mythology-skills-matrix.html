Skill Development Notes

  • MTS1 (Pattern Recognition) begins in Module 1 with creation patterns, builds through Hero's Journey analysis, and culminates in synthesizing patterns across all course content
  • MTS2 (Cultural Contextualization) progresses from ancient to modern contexts, helping students understand myths as products of their time and place
  • MTS3 (Comparative Analysis) develops students' ability to see connections across cultures, from ancient heroes to modern trickster figures
  • MTS4 (Symbolic Interpretation) teaches decoding of metaphorical language, from basic symbols to complex modern allegories
  • MTS5 (Contemporary Application) focuses on Modules 3-6, where students apply ancient patterns to Star Wars, LOTR, Harry Potter, and superheroes
  • MTS6 (Critical Analysis) culminates in Module 7-8, where students critically evaluate mythology's role in contemporary culture
