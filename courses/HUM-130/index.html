Course Overview

This course provides an in-depth study of myths and legends from ancient times through modern pop culture. Topics include the varied sources of myths and their influence on the individual and society within diverse cultural contexts.

1

Analyze the structure, function, and significance of myths across diverse cultures

2

Evaluate how myths reflect and shape cultural values, beliefs, and identities

3

Apply mythological archetypes and patterns to interpret modern narratives

4

Communicate mythological analysis through written and creative projects
